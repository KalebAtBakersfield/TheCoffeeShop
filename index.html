info About

The COFFEE House is run by Bakersfield students. Students can only have one cup of coffee a day and must pay when they receive their coffee. Free delivery to all staff! All high school students, staff, administration and teachers can buy coffee. $20 punch cards are available for purchase that will get you 10 cups of coffee and the 11th free. Students must pay daily but can still be eligible for punch cards. Extra sugar and specialty creamers are also avaliable.

Announcement

Due to unforeseen circumstances we will be selling limited items from the 17th to the 20th of October. We will be selling black coffee to faculty and staff only. There will be no sugars or creamers.
